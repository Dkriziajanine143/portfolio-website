MY HOBBIES :

Engaging in hobbies is a way to unwind, express creativity, and find joy in everyday activities. For me, hobbies like cooking, cleaning, and singing as a choir member in our chapel are not just pastimes but integral parts of my life that bring immense fulfillment and meaning. Cooking is a passion that allows me to explore new recipes and create delicious meals from scratch. The act of cooking is a form of self-expression that brings me immense satisfaction and joy. Cleaning, often seen as a mundane chore, holds a different significance for me. Tidying up my living space, organizing my belongings, and creating a serene environment through cleaning is a therapeutic practice that promotes mental clarity and a sense of order. Singing as a choir member in our chapel for almost 6 years is a hobby that holds a special place in my heart. The power of music to uplift spirits, inspire emotions, and foster a sense of community is truly remarkable. As part of the choir, I have the privilege of blending my voice with others, harmonizing melodies, and sharing songs of praise and worship. The unity, camaraderie, and shared passion for music create a bond that transcends individual voices, creating a harmonious blend that resonates with the soul and uplifts the spirit.
